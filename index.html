My Letter To You

Fadi, my munchkin…

I don’t know if you’ll ever read this, but I built this because I miss you deeply. December 8th, 2025 at 2:55 AM — that was the last moment I saw you, and my heart has counted every second since.

You’re soft, shy, stubborn, cute, jealous, kind, mysterious, funny, emotional — and somehow all of that melts me every time.

I miss the way you speak to me in that soft, playful voice. I miss the little ways you show you care even when you act like you don't. I miss your presence more than I can say — your comfort, your laugh, the way "my munchkin" makes you glow.

I love you, always,

Amin. ✨
